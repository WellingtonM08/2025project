About

Quick summary of being a barber in college

I found this passion of mine during the dark times of the pandemic.

Through a haircut I was able not only give a person their confidence and smile back, but I was able to understand them as a person.

It just wasn’t a haircut, it was confidence. Mixed with my passion of art I kept doing this to this day.

Since a child, art has always been my escape, whether it was middle school in art club making murals, or 8th grade taking piano classes.

Or if it was high school, where I would draw portraits of people, even the doodle drawings in class while the professor was giving a lecture.

Art has always been an escape, the only thing that has changed is the method in which I have done it, it’s now done through haircuts.

Balancing school and clients taught me time management, consistency, and attention to detail. Every appointment is a chance to get better at my craft.

Through this journey I have met amazing people of all aspects of life, from the family men laborer to the business men with a startup.

I got to understand not just what type of haircut they wanted but how they view life and what keeps them going.

It’s safe to say I love what I do because I do it to the best of my abilities, with care, great skill, and professionalism.
